Tongits Sabong Bingo Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to Tongits Sabong Bingo, a unique and engaging mobile game developed by Tongits Sabong Bingo. We are dedicated to protecting your privacy and ensuring a secure gaming experience. This Privacy Policy details how we handle the personal information of our users ("you") when you play our Game.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information: This includes details such as your full name, email address, and date of birth, which you provide during the account creation process.

In-Game Data: We record your gameplay progress, including levels completed, in-game purchases made, and time spent in various game stages.

Device Information: This covers specifics about your device, like model (e.g., iPhone 12), operating system (e.g., iOS 14), and unique identifiers like IMEI numbers.

Location Data: We collect both precise (GPS-based) and approximate (IP address-based) location data to offer location-specific events and to comply with regional regulations.

User and Device IDs: We assign unique identifiers to your game profile and device to maintain your game progress and enhance your gaming experience.

Cookies and Similar Technologies: These are used to remember your in-game settings and preferences, understand gameplay patterns, and support our marketing efforts.

3. Use of Your Information

To deliver and personalize your gaming experience, such as suggesting new levels based on your progress.

To communicate important updates, new features, or special offers tailored to your interests.

To analyze gameplay and user behavior to improve game design and user experience.

For security purposes, to enforce our terms, and to comply with legal obligations.

4. Sharing of Information

With Service Providers: We partner with third-party providers for cloud storage, data analytics, and customer support. These partners are contractually obligated to protect your information.

For Legal and Compliance Reasons: We may disclose your data when required by law, such as in response to court orders or legal investigations.

During Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale, we may transfer user information as part of the business assets.

5. Data Security

We use advanced security measures like encryption and firewalls to protect your data. Regular security audits are conducted to ensure the effectiveness of these measures.

Despite our efforts, please be aware that no security system is impervious to attack. We recommend that you also take steps to protect your account information.
